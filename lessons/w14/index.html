Tips

  • Use Google! Jue and I are here to help but search for your problem first before asking us.
  • Use jQuery to pull in your data but also feel free to use other JS libraries.
  • Using git commit your work often to keep files synced.
  • By the end of class you should have data on your page and decided what your interaction should be (search, filter, mapping, etc.)
