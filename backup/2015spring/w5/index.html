Meta Tags

What are meta tags?

  • Meta tags provide more information about your page to browers
  • They live in the head tag
  • Are useful for SEO purposes as search engines use them to grab more information about your site

Syntax

Constructed similar to link and img tags, with specific attributes depending on the type of information you want to include: <meta />

Most Common Types (SEO)

  • Description: Description of the page, often shown in search results
  • Robots: Indicates whether search engines should add this page to their results
  • Author: The author of the web page
  • Keywords: A list of words separated by commas that users might search to find your site*

Example: Description

<meta name="description" content="Profile website of Meghan Louttit, including her programming and design work at The New York Times, The Washington Post, and a blog about eating vegan in Brooklyn. />
