jsonp

  • Stands for "JSON with padding"
  • Wraps your JSON in a variable that can be accessed between domains
  • Pro: Cross-domain requests!!
  • Con: Weaker sercurity (only load JSONP from trusted sources) and less control over data requests
  • More detailed explanations here and here (if you're interested)
